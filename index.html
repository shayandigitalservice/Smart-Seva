About Smart Seva - India's Leading Home Service Network

Welcome to Smart Seva, your one-stop destination for all household requirements. In today's fast-paced world, finding reliable and skilled professionals like electricians, plumbers, and cleaning experts can be a daunting task. Smart Seva bridges the gap between verified service providers and customers looking for quality work at transparent prices. Whether you are located in Assam, Bihar, West Bengal, or any other region, our mission is to deliver excellence right to your doorstep.

Our platform specializes in wide range of services including residential deep cleaning, full-house electrical wiring, leak-proof plumbing solutions, and expert AC repair and maintenance. Every professional on our network undergoes a rigorous background check to ensure your safety and satisfaction. By choosing Smart Seva, you are opting for a hassle-free experience where booking takes less than a minute and the quality of work is guaranteed for 7 days.

For service professionals, Smart Seva offers a unique opportunity to grow their business. Our Worker Registration portal allows skilled laborers and technicians to join a platform that values their hard work and connects them with high-paying clients. Similarly, our Customer Registration ensures that every user gets personalized offers, loyalty points, and a dedicated dashboard to track their service history.
